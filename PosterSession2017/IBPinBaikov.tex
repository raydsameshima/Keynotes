\textbf{IBP in Baikov parametrization}

With Baikov parametrization, IBP identities become polynomial identities:
\begin{eqnarray}
\mathbf{O}_{ij}\left(\frac{\partial}{\partial x_a}, x_a \right) \left[P \right]^{\frac{d-M-1}{2}}
\end{eqnarray}
