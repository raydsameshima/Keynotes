Bez\"out's identity:
\begin{eqnarray}
\forall a,b \in \mathbb{N}, \exists x,y \in \mathbb{Z} \text{ s.t. } a*x + b*y = \gcd(a,b),
\end{eqnarray}
where $\gcd$ is the greatest common divisor.
