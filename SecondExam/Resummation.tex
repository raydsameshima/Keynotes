\begin{eqnarray*}
L &=& \ln\left( \frac{\text{"hard" scalse} }{\text{"soft" scale} }\right) \\
\alpha_s * L &\sim& 1
\end{eqnarray*}
So we need to expand an observable with respect to $\alpha_s$ \underline{and $L$} of certain combinations.
